Who we are

A kitchen with
deep roots

Zaffran started as a single tandoor oven in a South Chennai home kitchen. Today, the same family recipes — cooked with the same unhurried care — feed hundreds of people each evening.

We source our spices directly from farms in Kerala and Rajasthan, our bread dough is started fresh every morning, and nothing leaves our kitchen that we wouldn't serve at our own table.

— Meera & Arjun Krishnamurthy, Founders
